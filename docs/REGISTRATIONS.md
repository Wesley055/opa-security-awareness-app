\## Intellectual Property



\*\*(not started) Trademark Registration\*\*

Genuinely different from CAC — CAC registers the company entity name;

trademark protects the brand itself with exclusive nationwide rights

in a specific class of use, and stops others from using a confusingly

similar name for similar goods/services.

\- Register now, once filed: "OPA," "OPA Technologies Limited," and

&#x20; the logo once one is chosen (still an open item — see logo/brand

&#x20; mark note elsewhere in the project).

\- Do NOT register speculative future product names (e.g. anything

&#x20; Journey Intelligence-related) before they are genuinely being

&#x20; built — trademark filings require real or near-term intent to use

&#x20; the mark in commerce; registering names for deferred roadmap items

&#x20; wastes real money on something that isn't a real asset yet. Add

&#x20; specific product names to a filing only once they're real.

\- Cost: roughly ₦150,000–₦350,000 all-in per class, typically filed

&#x20; through a licensed agent. Realistic timeline: 8–12+ months

&#x20; (search, filing, examination, journal publication, opposition

&#x20; period, certificate issuance) — long enough that starting promptly

&#x20; after CAC completes is worthwhile, even though it shouldn't block

&#x20; anything else.

\- Owner: Charles | Next Action: Engage a trademark agent once CAC completes.



\*\*(not started) Copyright Strategy\*\*

Copyright exists automatically upon creation (source code,

documentation, UI/UX designs, marketing content) — no registration

required for it to legally exist. The Nigerian Copyright Commission

offers optional notification/eRegistration, which creates useful

dated evidence in case of a future dispute, but doesn't create the

right itself. Not a launch blocker.

\- Owner: Charles | Next Action: Consider NCC notification after MVP launch, not before.



\*\*(not started) Patent Review\*\*

Do not assume OPA needs a patent. Most software companies never

obtain one. Only worth exploring if a genuinely novel, patentable

method is developed — not simply because the product uses GPS,

notifications, or coordination logic. If something genuinely novel

emerges, consult a patent attorney \*before\* publicly disclosing

details, since public disclosure can affect patent rights in some

jurisdictions.

\- Owner: Charles | Next Action: None now. Reassess only if a genuinely novel invention emerges.



\*\*(ongoing) Trade Secrets\*\*

Protected through security practice and confidentiality, not

registration. Applies from day one to: detection/confidence-scoring

logic, any future AI models or prompts, risk-scoring algorithms,

responder-prioritization logic, infrastructure architecture,

deployment scripts, security procedures.

\- Owner: Charles | Next Action: Maintain access controls and

&#x20; confidentiality as a standing practice, not a one-time task.
